About SONG RATER:

SONG RATER is a new app that allows users to enter, rate, and review their favorite songs.
Using SONG RATER, you can share your musical hot takes with the world.
Developed by students at Wesleyan University, SONG RATER is committed to creating a community of music-lovers.


Share playlists (like the one above!) with friends, and enter your ratings on SONG RATER to share your music taste with the world.

This site was designed and published as part of the COMP 333 Software Engineering class at Wesleyan University.
The described platform is not publicly available but rather is created as a training exercise.
